Your workday as a timeline

Planifie brings timers, notes, and documents together into a per-task timeline. Organize everything in workspaces, label with tags, and export whenever you want — no cloud, your data stays with you.

Three blocks, one timeline

Each task is a timeline built from the blocks you need, in the order they happened.

◷

Timer

Start, pause, resume, and finish labeled timers. A floating bar shows the active timer and takes you straight to it. Save presets for the timers you use most.

✎

Note

Capture ideas and reminders in seconds. Short notes are recorded at the exact moment they came up, right inside the task's timeline.

▤

Document

A rich editor (BlockNote) with headings, lists, blocks, and formatting. Open it fullscreen to write specs, meeting notes, and longer entries.

Organization that keeps your pace

Split contexts into workspaces, find any task with search, and add color to things with tags.

  • ✓ Color-coded workspaces to separate projects, clients, or areas of life.
  • ✓ Tags with colors to classify and filter your tasks.
  • ✓ Instant search in the sidebar to get exactly where you need.
  • ✓ Light and dark themes in a warm palette, easy on the eyes.

Your data, your way

Local-first by default, with clean export to take your content anywhere.

⤓

Export to Markdown

Turn an entire task — or a single document — into Markdown ready to use in any editor.

▦

Export to PDF

Generate PDFs with real selectable text (not an image), preserving formatting of headings, lists, and code.

⛁

Local storage

Everything lives in a SQLite database on your machine via Tauri. No account, no cloud, no tracking. Fast and private.
